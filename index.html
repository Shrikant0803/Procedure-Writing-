Steps to Create a WhatsApp Group (Android)

1. Open WhatsApp:

a) Unlock your Android device and locate the WhatsApp app icon.

b) Tap the icon to open the app.

2. Access the Chats Tab:

By default, you'll land on the "Chats" tab.

NOTE: If not, tap on the chat bubble icon at the bottom to navigate there.

3. Initiate Group Creation:

Click on the three dots.

NOTE: Alternate option is look for the "New Chat" button, often represented by a pencil or speech bubble icon. Tap it to start creating a new chat.

4. Start a New Group:

Click on the "New Group" option that appears when you click on the "New Group" button.

5. Select Participants:

a) You will see a list of contacts. Tap the ones you want to add to the group.

b) Selected contacts will have a checkmark next to their names. You can choose multiple contacts.

6. Create the Group:

a) Once all participants are selected, tap "Next" or "Create Group."

b) WhatsApp will create the group with the selected participants.

7. Customize Group Settings (Optional):

a) After creation, you can customize the group settings.

b) Set a group name, add a group photo, and adjust privacy settings as needed.

c) Tap the group's name or the camera icon next to it to make changes.

TIP: Choose group participants wisely for meaningful conversations. Assign a descriptive group name and photo for easy identification.

8. Start Chatting:

a) You'll be redirected to the group chat window.

b) Type your message and tap send to share it with the group.
